Delicious Caldereta Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1kg. Pork Spareribs
  • 2 Sachets Magic Sarap
  • 2 tbls Vegetable Oil
  • 6 Cloves Garlic
  • 1 Piece Onion
  • 1 Piece Red Bell Pepper
  • 1 Piece Green Bell Pepper
  • 1 Piece Chorizo De Bilbao
  • 1 Piece Bay Leaf/li>
  • 0.5 Cup Tomato Paste
  • 1 Can LIverwurst Spread
  • 2 Cups Water
  • 2 tbls Brown Sugar
  • 0.5 Cup Coconut Cream
  • 0.25 Cup Cheese

Instructions

  1. For 2 minutes, Season pork with MAGIC SARAP. Saute in oil until golden brown. Remove and set aside.
  2. For 5 minutes, Saute garlic, onion, nell peppers, chili, chorizo, tomato paste and liver spread.
  3. For 60 minutes, Pour water, add pork, bay leaf, and sugar. Simmer for 1 hour.
  4. For 3 minutes, Season with remaining sachet of Magic Sarap. Stir in coconut cream and cheese. Transfer into a serving plate and serve
