Delicious Sinigang Recipe

Ingredients

  • 5 cups Water
  • 0.5 kg Pork Spareribs
  • 1 pc Onion
  • 2 pcs Tomato
  • 1 pc Finger Chili
  • 0.5 cup Radish
  • 0.5 cup Okra
  • 0.5 cup Sitaw
  • 1 sachet Sinigang Sampalok With Gabi Mix
  • 1 cup Kangkong

Instructions

  1. 50 Min. Simmer pork with water with onion and tomatoes for 45 minutes.
  2. 8 Min. Add siling panigang, radish, okra and sitaw with 2-min intervals.
  3. 2 Min. Pour MAGGI Magic Sinigang with Gabi. Stir in kangkong. Transfer into a serving bowl and serve immediately.
