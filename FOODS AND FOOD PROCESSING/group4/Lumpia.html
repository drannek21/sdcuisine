Delicious Lumpiang Shanghai Recipe

Ingredients

  • 0.5 kg Ground Pork
  • 1 pc Onion
  • 1 pc Carrot
  • 1 sachet MAGGI Oyster Sauce
  • 1 sachet Magic Sarap
  • 20 Lumpia Wrapper
  • 1 cup Vegetable Oil
  • 1 cup Water
  • 2 tbsp MAGGI Oyster Sauce
  • 4 cloves Garlic
  • 1 pc Red And Green Chili
  • 1 tbsp Brown Sugar
  • 0.25 tsp freshly ground pepper
  • 1 tbsp Cornstarch

Instructions

  1. 20 Min. Combine ground pork, onion, carrot, MAGGI Oyster Sauce and ½ sachet of MAGGI Magic Sarap. Mix well. Assemble 1 - 1½ tbsp per lumpia wrapper.
  2. 15 Min. Preheat oil and fry lumpia until golden brown. Transfer on cooling rack to drain excess oil.
  3. 5 Min. To prepare the dipping sauce, mix water with MAGGI Oyster Sauce, ½ sachet of MAGGI Magic Sarap, garlic, chili, sugar and pepper. Bring to simmer.
  4. 5 Min. Stir in cornstarch mixture to thicken the sauce. Transfer into a serving bowl. Serve with freshly cooked Lumpiang Shanghai.
